Plan by Season

The Perfect Time
for Every Traveller

Konkan transforms dramatically with seasons. Each time of year offers something truly special — pick what excites you most.

🌧️
June – September
Monsoon Magic
The Konkan transforms into a lush paradise. Every hillside is emerald green, streams overflow, and waterfalls roar to life. Perfect for nature lovers and those who love the drama of rain.
🌊 Waterfalls🌿 Green Trails💧 Streams🌧️ Rain Walks
🌤️
October – February
Winter Bliss
Ideal weather — clear skies, gentle breeze, perfect for beach walks, fort treks, and snorkeling. The most popular season with families and groups. All activities fully open.
🏖️ Beaches🏰 Forts🐠 Snorkeling🚤 Boat Rides
☀️
March – May
Mango & Beaches
The season of Alphonso mangoes! Visit orchards, enjoy fresh seafood, and explore quiet beaches before the crowds arrive. Hot but rewarding for the adventurous.
🥭 Mango Tours🐟 Seafood🌅 Sunsets🏄 Surfing

Ask Us →
🏖️
Ratnagiri
~340 km from Mumbai
The gem of Konkan. Home to Ratnadurg Fort, Ganpatipule Beach, Thiba Palace, and the famous Alphonso mango orchards. Rich history and stunning coastal scenery.
Ratnadurg Fort Ganpatipule Mango Orchards Seafood
🥭
Devgad
~490 km from Mumbai
Famous for the world's finest Alphonso mangoes (GI certified) and pristine Devgad Beach. A quiet town with a historic fort, lighthouse, and peaceful countryside.
Hapus Orchards Devgad Beach Devgad Fort Local Cuisine
🐠
Malvan
~520 km from Mumbai
Snorkeling capital of Maharashtra! Crystal clear waters at Tarkarli, the magnificent Sindhudurg Fort on an island, and the spicy, flavourful Malvani cuisine.
Tarkarli Beach Sindhudurg Snorkeling Malvani Food
🌅
Vengurla
~570 km from Mumbai
The hidden gem of Konkan. Beautiful Vengurla Rocks island, cashew forests, Sagareshwar Beach, and a Portuguese-era lighthouse. Least crowded and most peaceful.
Vengurla Rocks Sagareshwar Lighthouse Cashew Forest
🏰
Sindhudurg District
The southern treasure belt
A district of wonders — sea forts, backwaters, mangrove trails, dolphin spotting, and tribal culture. Includes Malvan, Vengurla, Kankavli, and the Amboli hill station.
Sea Forts Amboli Falls Backwaters Dolphins
